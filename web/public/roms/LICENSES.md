# Bundled game ROM licenses

These homebrew NES games are bundled with NES Studio purely as ready-to-run
demos. They are **not** part of the emulator and each is redistributed under its
own free license, verified 2026-07-06. All were authored by **Shiru**
(https://shiru.untergrund.net/).

| Game | File | Author | License | Source |
|------|------|--------|---------|--------|
| Lawn Mower | `lawn-mower.nes` | Shiru | [CC0 1.0 (public domain)](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/) | https://archive.org/details/lawn_mower · https://shiru.untergrund.net/software.shtml |
| Chase | `chase.nes` | Shiru | [CC0 1.0 (public domain)](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/) | https://archive.org/details/chase_nes_example_code · https://shiru.untergrund.net/software.shtml |
| Lan Master | `lan-master.nes` | Shiru | [CC0 1.0 (public domain)](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/) | https://shiru.untergrund.net/software.shtml · https://forums.nesdev.org/viewtopic.php?t=10404 |
| Zooming Secretary | `zooming-secretary.nes` | Shiru & PinWizz | [CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/) | https://shiru.untergrund.net/software.shtml · https://forums.nesdev.org/viewtopic.php?t=10404 |

## Notes
- **CC0** titles are dedicated to the public domain — no attribution required, but
  we credit the author anyway (see the in-app Help dialog).
- **Zooming Secretary** is CC BY 4.0, which *requires* attribution. Credit is given
  to Shiru & PinWizz in the in-app Help dialog and here.
- The community list of freely-redistributable NES homebrew used to confirm these:
  https://forums.nesdev.org/viewtopic.php?t=10404
